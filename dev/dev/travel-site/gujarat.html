Gujarat: deserts, temples, coastlines

From the Great Rann to Somnath and Gir’s lions—discover the west coast’s wonders.

Gujarat – Places Worth Seeing

A vibrant state on India's western coast—rich in history, wildlife, crafts, and coastline.

  • Best time Oct–Mar (pleasant weather)
  • Airports Ahmedabad, Vadodara, Rajkot, Bhavnagar, Kandla, Porbandar, Jamnagar

Nature & Wildlife

Gir National Park

Home to the Asiatic lion. Book safaris in advance (Sasan Gir). Best time: Dec–Apr.

Great Rann of Kutch

Otherworldly white salt desert. Visit during Rann Utsav (Nov–Feb) for tents, music, and moonlit views.

Marine National Park, Jamnagar

India’s first marine park. Low-tide walks reveal corals and starfish near Narara Island (check timings).

Coastal & Pilgrimage

Dwarka

One of the Char Dham. Dwarkadhish Temple and coastal views; take a side trip to Bet Dwarka.

Somnath

Historic Shiva jyotirlinga on the Arabian Sea. Evening aarti and sound-and-light show are highlights.

Porbandar & Coast

Birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi; calm beaches along the Saurashtra coast make for relaxed sunsets.

Culture & Crafts

Food Trail

From Kathiyawadi thali to Khaman, Dhokla, Khandvi, and street-side Fafda‑Jalebi—come hungry!

Kutch Handicrafts

Ajrakh block prints, mirror work, and leather crafts—visit Bhujodi and Hodka artisan villages.

Navratri in Gujarat

Garba nights in Ahmedabad and Vadodara are electric. Traditional attire recommended; events run till late.
